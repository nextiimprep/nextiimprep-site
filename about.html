About NextIIM Prep

Next Begins Here
Where IIM Dreams Take Their First Step

NextIIM Prep is a mission-driven initiative designed to help aspirants crack India’s top management entrance exams after Class 12. We are building the most student-centric, affordable, and premium preparation platform, focused on exams like IPMAT, JIPMAT, DU JAT, SET, NPAT, Christ, and more.

Our Vision

To democratize access to IIMs and top B-schools for every student – regardless of location, background, or affordability.

Our Mission

To offer India's best guidance, content, and mentorship for UG Management Entrance exams through innovative content, top mentors, and a supportive ecosystem.

About the Founder

Pragesh Chandra is the Founder & Chief Mentor at NextIIM Prep, with 11+ years of experience in education, operations, and academic leadership. An alumnus of IIM Kashipur, he has served in director-level roles at Sankalp Bharat, Adda247, Mahendras, and Career Launcher.

A former Software Engineer at Lenovo India, he has cracked exams like GATE, CAT, GMAT, SSC CGL, IBPS, and more. His vision through NextIIM is to make UG management prep more accessible and student-focused.
