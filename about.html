About NextIIM Prep

NextIIM Prep is a mission-driven initiative designed to help aspirants crack India’s top management entrance exams after Class 12. We are building the most student-centric, affordable, and premium preparation platform, focused on exams like IPMAT, JIPMAT, DU JAT, SET, NPAT, Christ, and more.

Our Vision

To democratize access to IIMs and top B-schools for every student – regardless of location, background, or affordability.

Our Mission

To offer India's best guidance, content, and mentorship for UG Management Entrance exams through innovative content, top mentors, and a supportive ecosystem.

About the Founder

Pragesh Chandra is the Founder & Chief Mentor at NextIIM Prep, India’s emerging powerhouse in UG management entrance preparation. An alumnus of IIM Kashipur, Pragesh brings over 11 years of rich experience in education, academic operations, and business leadership.

Before founding NextIIM, he held Director-level roles at leading education brands like Sankalp Bharat, Adda247, Mahendras, and Career Launcher, where he helped scale multiple verticals and impact thousands of student journeys.

A former Software Engineer at Lenovo India, Pragesh made a career shift driven by his passion for teaching. He has successfully cracked top competitive exams including GATE, CAT, GMAT, SSC CGL, IBPS, and more—making him uniquely relatable to aspirants.

Today, with NextIIM, he is on a mission to democratize access to quality UG management prep and nurture India’s future IIM leaders through personalized mentorship, strategic guidance, and a results-driven ecosystem.
