What is IPM?

Integrated Program in Management (IPM) is a 5-year course that offers direct entry into top IIMs after Class 12 through exams like IPMAT and JIPMAT, without the need for CAT. Several leading B-Schools like NMIMS, Symbiosis, Nirma, and Christ also offer similar integrated MBA programs via their own entrance tests.
