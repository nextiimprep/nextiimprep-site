About NextIIM Prep

NextIIM Prep is a mission-driven initiative designed to help aspirants crack India’s top management entrance exams after Class 12. We are building the most student-centric, affordable, and premium preparation platform, focused on exams like IPMAT, JIPMAT, DU JAT, SET, NPAT, Christ, and more.

Our Vision

To democratize access to IIMs and top B-schools for every student – regardless of location, background, or affordability.

Our Mission

Building India's most trusted learning platform that delivers affordable, high-quality education and job-ready skills in local languages—helping every learner in Bharat learn, grow, and succeed from anywhere.

Meet the Founder

Pragesh Chandra, the visionary behind NextIIM, is an alumnus of IIM Kashipur with a decade-long leadership background across leading EdTech giants. He left his director-level role to launch this student-first initiative, blending affordability with mentorship. A former software engineer turned teacher and mentor, he has cracked GATE, CAT, GMAT, SSC, IBPS and more. His goal: bring premium UG management preparation to every ambitious student in India.
